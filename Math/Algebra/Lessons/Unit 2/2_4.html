Lesson 4: Interpreting Linear Graphs and Tables

The graph of the linear function $f(x)$ in the standard $xy$-plane has a positive slope and a negative $y$-intercept. Which of the following quadrants on the graph must contain points that satisfy the function $f(x)$?

A researcher records the remaining volume of a liquid sample inside a lab container over several hours. The collected data is displayed in the table below.

Hours ($t$) Volume in Milliliters ($V$)
$2$ $160$
$5$ $115$
$8$ $70$

Based on the linear relationship shown in the table, what is the volume of the sample, in milliliters, at 0 hours?

In the standard $xy$-plane, the graph of a linear function passes through the point $(0, b)$ and has a slope of $m$. If a table representing this function shows that an increase of 3 units in $x$ results in a decrease of 12 units in $y$, what is the value of $m$?

A line graphed in the standard $xy$-plane has a horizontal intercept ($x$-intercept) at $(-6, 0)$ and a vertical intercept ($y$-intercept) at $(0, 4)$. What is the slope of this line?
