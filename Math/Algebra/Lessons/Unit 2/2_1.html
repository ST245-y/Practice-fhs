Lesson 1: Linear Equations in Two Variables: Meaning and Structure

$$C = 1.50m + 3.50$$A taxi service uses the given equation to calculate the total cost, $C$, in dollars, for a ride of $m$ miles. What is the best interpretation of the number $3.50$ in this context?

$$4.5x + 2.5y = 90$$A school club sells $x$ boxes of popcorn and $y$ drinks at a sporting event to raise money. The club goal is modeled by the given equation. What does the coefficient $4.5$ represent in this context?

$$W = -0.4d + 12$$An environmental study models the total weight of a specific glacier, $W$, in millions of tons, where $d$ represents the number of days since the start of spring. What is the best interpretation of the statement 'the $d$-intercept of the graph of this equation is 30'?

$$P = 28.5 + 0.75h$$A structural engineer calculates the internal pressure, $P$, in pounds per square inch (psi), of a foundational column after it has been submerged in water for $h$ hours. What is the best interpretation of the number $0.75$ in this equation?

$$15A + 9S = 4500$$The given equation models the ticket revenue for an evening theater performance, where $A$ is the number of adult tickets sold and $S$ is the number of student tickets sold. If the graph of this equation is plotted on an $AS$-plane where $A$ is on the horizontal axis, what does the vertical intercept of the graph represent?

$$y = 35x + 120$$A commercial repair technician uses the given equation to model the total charge, $y$, in dollars, for a service call taking $x$ hours. If the technician modifies the pricing structure to increase the base diagnostic fee by $\$40$ and lower the hourly rate by $\$5$, what is the expression for the new total charge?

$$V = 14000 - 1850t$$A company monitors the depreciation of a delivery vehicle using the given linear model, where $V$ represents the value of the vehicle, in dollars, $t$ years after it was purchased. Which statement is supported by the structure of the model?

$$ax + by = c$$In the given equation, $a$, $b$, and $c$ are positive constants. If this equation is graphed in the standard $xy$-plane, which of the following statements about the graph must be true?

$$y = m(x - 4) + 10$$In the given equation, $m$ is a positive constant. If this equation is graphed in the standard $xy$-plane, which of the following geometric interpretations of its structural form is correct?

An oceanographic research team measures water temperature at various depths. They determine that the relationship is linear and can be modeled by the equation $T = -0.08d + k$, where $T$ is the temperature in degrees Celsius, $d$ is the depth below the surface in meters, and $k$ is a constant. If the temperature decreases by $1.2$ degrees Celsius for every increase of $15$ meters in depth, and the temperature at a depth of 50 meters is 18 degrees Celsius, what is the value of $k$?
