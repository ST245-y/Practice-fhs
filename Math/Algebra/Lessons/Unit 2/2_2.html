Lesson 1: Linear Equations in Two Variables: Meaning and Structure

A construction crew is paving a road at a constant rate. At 9:00 AM, they have paved 120 meters of the road. By 1:00 PM on the same day, they have paved a total of 200 meters. What is the crew's paving rate, in meters per hour?

The graph of the linear function $f(x) = -4.5x + 60$ models the remaining battery percentage of a laptop after $x$ hours of continuous use. Which statement best describes the slope of the graph?

A commercial printing press prints brochures at a constant rate. The table below shows the total number of brochures printed, $B$, after a given number of minutes, $m$.

Minutes ($m$) Brochures ($B$)
$4$ $700$
$7$ $1,150$
$11$ $1,750$

A manufacturing plant uses a large fluid tank during production. The volume of liquid in the tank decreases at a constant rate over time. If the tank contains 1,200 gallons after 3 hours of operation and 900 gallons after 7 hours of operation, what is the rate of change of the liquid volume, in gallons per hour?

An e-commerce business tracks its total monthly operational costs using a linear model. If the company ships 1,500 packages in June, its total cost is $\$8,250$. If it ships 2,200 packages in July, its total cost is $\$10,350$. What is the marginal cost to ship each additional package, in dollars?

A dynamic fitness application monitors a runner's pace during a workout. The runner's distance from the starting line increases linearly according to the equation $d = r \cdot t + b$, where $d$ is the distance in meters, $t$ is the time in seconds, $r$ is the constant running rate, and $b$ is a constant. If the runner travels 18 meters between $t = 4$ seconds and $t = 7$ seconds, what is the value of $r$?

A tech startup launches a mobile application and records the total number of active users over its first few weeks. The relationship between the number of weeks since launch, $w$, and the total active users, $U$, is modeled by a linear equation. If the app has 3,400 active users at week 2 and gains exactly 450 new active users each week, which equation accurately models this relationship?

A researcher records the temperature of a chemical compound during a cooling experiment. The temperature drops at a constant rate of $0.15$ degrees Celsius per second. Which of the following values represents the rate of change of the compound's temperature in degrees Celsius per minute?

In the standard $xy$-plane, a line passes through the points $(2k, 5)$ and $(6k, 17)$, where $k$ is a non-zero constant. Which of the following expressions represents the slope of this line in terms of $k$?

An agricultural drone dispenses fertilizer over a crop field. The volume of fertilizer remaining in the drone's tank, $V$, in liters, is modeled by a linear function of the flight time, $m$, in minutes. The drone begins its flight with a full tank and operates until the tank is completely empty. If the drone has 48 liters remaining after 6 minutes of flight, and 20 liters remaining after 13 minutes of flight, how many minutes total does it take for the full fertilizer tank to drain completely?
